What Exactly Is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. In simple terms, it is the process of making your website appear on Google (and other search engines) when people search for products or services you offer.

Think about the last time you searched for something online. You probably clicked one of the first few results. You didn't scroll to page three — almost nobody does. SEO is what helps businesses reach that first page and stay there.
